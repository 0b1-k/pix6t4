Pete Brown assembles his PIX-6T4 and tells the world about his experience

Tags: Pete Brown, PIX-6T4, pix6t4, Unboxing

Pete Brown just bought a PIX-6T4 and tells about his experience, from unboxing the kit, all the way to writing his first "Hello World!" application.

If you've been thinking about getting in on the PIX-6T4 fun, you should really read his post: http://10rem.net/blog/2012/01/21/assembling-the-pix-6t4-netduino-powered-hand-held-game-system :)

Pete follows-up with an excellent PIX-6T4 game development tutorial and shows the process he followed to whip up a racing game in one evening.
