Pete Brown's tutorial on creating his 'Sixty4Racer' game for the PIX-6T4

In a great follow-up to his previous article on assembling the PIX-6T4, Pete Brown puts the console's rapid game development concept to the test and whips up a racing game in one evening. Pete does a fantastic job at explaining the design process step-by-step, starting from the game concept all the way to creating  a standalone game 'cartridge' for all of us to enjoy.

Awesome job Pete and thanks for sharing this excellent tutorial.
